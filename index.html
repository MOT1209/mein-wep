About Rashid

Who is Rashid?

I am Rashid (MOT1209), a Full-Stack Developer and AI Engineer specialized in building dynamic, immersive web platforms. My journey started with curiosity — manipulating CSS, breaking JavaScript, rebuilding everything better. Now I build AI-powered tools, 3D games, and full-stack applications used by thousands.

What do I build?

AI assistants powered by Gemini & OpenRouter, 3D sandbox worlds with Three.js, full-stack platforms with Next.js 15 + Supabase, and open-source tools that help developers ship faster. Every project is a PWA — installable, offline-ready, and performant.

Mission

Democratizing AI and 3D web development through open-source tools, educational content, and community-driven platforms. I believe technology should empower everyone — from solo devs to global teams.
